Profile

In my Final Year Project, I have worked on "Visualizing the impact of Therapy on Stroke: 3D Ultrasound Measurement for Enhanced Assessment". For this project, I have applied techniques such as medical image preprocessing and machine learning to analyze ultrasound data and visualize how therapy impacts stroke recovery over time. This will help clinicians better assess patient progress and tailor rehabilitation plans more effectively.
